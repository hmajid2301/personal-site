## Background

You can adjust the background colour of the header by passing the `background` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Background">
    <div>
      <Header
        background="gray-200"
        logo={<Logo text="Haseeb" />}
        links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Project', 'Open Source']}
      />
      <Header
        background="blue-500"

    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Collapse Breakpoint Class

You can adjust the point where the nav bar breaks into "mobile" mode by passing the `collapseBreakpointClass` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Collapse Breakpoint Class">
    <div>
      <Header

        logo={<Logo text="Haseeb" />}
        links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Project', 'Open Source']}
      />
      <Header
        collapseBreakpointClass="md"
        logo={<Logo text="Haseeb" />}
        links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Project', 'Open Source']}
      />
      <Header
        collapseBreakpointClass="lg"
        logo={<Logo a text="Haseeb" />}
        links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Project', 'Open Source']}
      />
      <Header
        collapseBreakpointClass="xl"
        logo={<Logo text="Haseeb" />}
        links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Project', 'Open Source']}
      />
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
