## Hover Color

You can change the hover colour of the link text by passing the `hoverColor` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Hover Colour">
    <Links
      hoverColor="purple-500"
      links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Projects', 'Open Source']}
    />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Color

You can colour of the text `color` prop.

<Preview>

    <Links
      color="green-700"
      links={['Home', 'Blog', 'Projects', 'Open Source']}
    />
  </Story>
</Preview>
