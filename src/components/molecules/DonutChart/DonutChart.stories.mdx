## Length

You can adjust the length of the items `length` prop, this is used to work out the average.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Length">
    <DonutChart
      length={20}
      title="Average word count"
      total={3700}
      unit="words"
    />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Title

You can adjust the title of the chart by passing the `title` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Title">
    <DonutChart length={10} title="Average chart" total={3700} unit="words" />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Total

You can adjust the total number of the elements (added together) `total` prop, this is used
to work out the average.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Total">
    <DonutChart
      length={10}
      title="Average word count"
      total={1800}
      unit="words"
    />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Unit

You can adjust the unit shown by passing the `unit` prop.
