## Background

You can adjust the background colour of the header by passing the `background` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Background">
    <div>
      <DesktopNavbar background="gray-200" />
      <DesktopNavbar
        background="blue-500"
        linkColor="white"
        hoverColor="gray-200"
      />
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Color

You can change the link colour by passing the `color` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Color">
    <div>
      <DesktopNavbar color="gray-600" />
      <DesktopNavbar color="orange-600" />
      <DesktopNavbar color="purple-500" />
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Hover Color

You can change the hover colour of the link text by passing the `hoverColor` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Hover Color">
    <div>
      <DesktopNavbar hoverColor="gray-500" />
      <DesktopNavbar hoverColor="purple-500" />
      <DesktopNavbar hoverColor="green-500" />
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Links

You can change the links shown by passing the `link` prop.
