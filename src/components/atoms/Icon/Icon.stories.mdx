## Background

You change the icon background by passing the `background` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Background">
    <Icon background="red-500" icon={<Sun />} />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## Icon

You can the icon by passing the `icon` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Icon">
    <Icon icon={<Sun />} />
  </Story>
</Preview>

## OnClick

You can call a function onClick by passing the `onClick` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="OnClick">
    <Icon icon={<Sun />} onClick={() => console.log('Hello!')} />
  </Story>
</Preview>
